Join your neighbors on Saturday, December 7th at 9:30 am to plant trees at James Marshall Park! We will be planting about 10 trees to replace some that have come down over the last few years. Bring a lunch and stay after to socialize and discuss other opportunities to help make Sacramento a greener and more beautiful city.

Do you have a space on your property near Marshall Park where you’d like a tree? Reach out to us and we can plant a tree for you during the event!
