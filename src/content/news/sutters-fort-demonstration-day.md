Join California State Parks and Friends of Sutter’s Fort for an informative (and fun!) Demonstration Day at Sutter’s Fort State Historic Park on Saturday, July 20th.

During this event (which is part of a series this summer) you can view or participate in a variety of activities – many of which are also part of the popular school field trip programs – such as hands-on stations, volunteer demonstrations, and staff-led presentations.

As a special bonus, visitors during Demonstration Days will also enjoy complimentary admission to the State Indian Museum!

Don’t miss this unique opportunity to learn, participate, and have fun with your family and friends. We look forward to seeing you there!
