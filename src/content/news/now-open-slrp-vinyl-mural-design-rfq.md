The City of Sacramento, Office of Arts + Culture invites professional Artists, illustrators, and graphic Designers to submit their qualifications for the design of an exterior 5000 sq. ft. vinyl mural at Sutter’s Landing Regional Park Baler Building. 

Artists may apply separately or as a team; additional consideration will be given to artists who have prior experience with or connections to the American River and Sacramento area. **The deadline to apply is July 25**.An information session will take place June 28, 5:30pm.
