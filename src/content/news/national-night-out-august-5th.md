Celebrated on the first Tuesday of August each year, National Night Out was launched in 1984 with the goal of bringing communities together and strengthening relationships between residents and the local police. There will be block parties, festivals, cookouts, and other public events throughout Sacramento this year. The Boulevard Park and Marshall-New Era Neighborhood Associations will co-host a progressive potluck dinner with three courses at three different locations. Everyone is invited!

Here is the schedule for our event: Appetizer course on the 22nd Street median between G Street and Fat Alley (6:00 p.m.); dinner course in the courtyard beside 2431 H Street (7:00 p.m.); and dessert course near the tot-lot in Marshall Park, 28th and I Streets (8:00 p.m.). Bring a favorite dish to share at any one of the three courses. Beverages will be provided. Guests from outside our neighborhood will include City officials, elected representatives, and members of the police department.

Click for a [flyer](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1vgsj70paqU-40LH2kmizu0k4haCEeByP/view?usp=sharing) about the event that you can share with friends and neighbors.
