Residents are invited to share their thoughts, concerns, and suggestions on our nighttime economy for the Sacramento Sociable City Assessment. The City’s Office of Nighttime Economy is partnering with Responsible Hospitality Institute (RHI), a non-profit dedicated to working with cities to build safe, vibrant, and well-managed social economies, to develop a nighttime management plan to enhance our nightlife and social economy.

Wednesday, May 17 at 5:30 pmTsakopoulos Library Galleria – 828 I Street

Nighttime businesses are part of our successful and vibrant nightlife, but they can impact the quality of life for neighborhoods. We want to hear from residents to help us establish priorities and goals. Please share the attached event flyer and encourage your members to join us for this meeting to provide their insight.

If you have any questions or need more information, please contact feel free to contact me. And please visit: www.sociablecity.solutions/sacramento/home for more details on the assessment.
