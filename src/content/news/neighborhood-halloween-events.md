Save the Date: Neighborhood Halloween Movie Night & Trunk-or-Treat!

The Boulevard Park Neighborhood Association (BPNA) and Marshall-New Era Neighborhood Association (MNENA) are joining to bring you a special Halloween event on Saturday, October 26th at the Marshall School! Join us for a spooky trunk-or-treat replete with games, activities, and snacks, followed by a haunted outdoor (family-friendly!) movie night. More details and updates will be shared via flyers, e-mail, and the BPNA Facebook page. Volunteers are needed! Please contact Noah Mebane at mebanen@gmail.com or 302-943-7445 if interested.
