Get some exercise and meet your neighbors while caring for our community!  **WHEN** – July  29, 9:00 am-11:00am. Sign in and coffee & pastries from 8:30am-9:00am.  **WHERE** – Stanford Park on the corner of 28th and B Streets. **HOW** – We will walk our neighborhood picking up trash. We have trash pickers available for use or you can bring your own. Trash bags and gloves will be provided.  We would love to have you come out and improve our neighborhood! Please let us know if you plan on attending.
