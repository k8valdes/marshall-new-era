Native Planting @ Sutter’s Landing  
Saturday, February 28th | 9:30 AM  
Join your neighbors and ReWild Sacramento on Saturday, February 28th at 9:30 AM to bring native plants back to Sutter’s Landing. We will also lay mulch, pull invasive weeds, and water the wildflowers we planted in our last event.

[Click here](https://boulevardpark.us15.list-manage.com/track/click?u=5e0f53affca0840d7ed176811&id=890f769e77&e=838f37540a) to register for the event and get updates in the event of cancellation.

We will meet in the parking lot for the skate park, at the end of the road. Make sure to bring clothing suitable for the weather which can include cold, wind, rain or sun. It’s also recommended to bring a water bottle, gloves, and any tools you may find helpful. For questions, contact planttrees@rewildsacramento.org.
