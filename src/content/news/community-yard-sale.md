Join your neighbors on Saturday, June 7th from 9:00 am to 1:00 pm at Washington Elementary School (520 18th St.). There will be a community wide yard sale with two options: sell from a stall at the flea market or sell in front of your own house that day and have it advertised on a yard sale map ($ 10 fee). To sign up go to [boulevardpark.org/yardsale](https://www.boulevardpark.org/yardsale).
