Our next MNENA Board Meeting is Monday July 10th at 6:00 pm.  We’ll meet at the Society Church, 2711 B St, Sacramento (where B Street Theater used to be).  They have been very kind to offer their building for our meetings.

As usual we will have a light potluck before we start the meeting so please bring something to share if you like, or just bring your questions and socialize with us.  Masks are not required but feel free to wear one if you are more comfortable doing so.  No need to RSVP, just show up.  

Here is a [draft agenda](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1mCZDqQxLu3Smgyr82zdBTdWg6gBI3W_K/view) and the draft minutes from the [May meeting](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1WcCYYgPdDkzEjbsQ8X1zH2jADXTRSEVR/view).

Hope to see you there,

The MNENA Board
