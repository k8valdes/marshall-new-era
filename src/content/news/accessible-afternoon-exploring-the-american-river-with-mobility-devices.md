Participants and volunteers…. The Lionheart Community presents an afternoon exploring the American River with NatureTrack, McIllwain Mobility, and Paraboards.

Saturday, November 9, 2024 12:30-4:30PM  
Sutter’s Landing Regional Park front of Skate Park  
20 28th St, Sacramento, CA 95816  
FREE EVENT!

Get ready for an epic day outdoors! Experience the thrill of hitting the trails and beach with NatureTrack’s fleet of Freedom Tracks! Plus, test drive McIlwain Mobility’s incredible lineup – from the rugged Eco Rover and Action Track Chair as well as the OMEO to the mind-blowing SwinCars. Catch speed in your manual chair with the new Paraboards! Don’t miss out!

Reserve your time slot on Eventbrite if you are interested in using the Freedom Trax with your manual wheelchair. All other equipment can be tried on a first come, first served basis. https://www.eventbrite.com/e/access-the-american-river-with-accessible-mobility-devices-tickets-1039379932357
