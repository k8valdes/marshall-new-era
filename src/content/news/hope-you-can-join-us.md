Get some exercise and meet your neighbors while caring for our community!  

**WHEN** – Saturday July 20th 9:00 am-11:00am; coffee & pastries from 8:30am-9:00am.    
**WHERE** – Society Church at Leland Stanford Park, 27th and B Street

We have trash pickers available for use or you can bring your own. Trash bags and gloves will be provided.  

We would love to have you come out and improve our neighborhood!   
**Invite your friends and neighbors to join us!**
