Join your neighborhood associations for our Halloween Block Party! Family friendly activities for all ages. Music, food, and games. Meet your neighbors and make new friends. Learn about what’s going on in our community, get involved, and celebrate with us.

Admission is FREE!

**Hosted By**: Marshall-New Era & Boulevard Park Neighborhood Associations

**Who**: All residents are welcome! You do not need to be a member of the neighborhood associations to attend.

**Date**: Saturday, October 18th

**Time**: 3:00 pm to 7:00 pm

**Location**: 21st and G Streets

**Volunteer**: We need volunteers! Click [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd16et5q7n6WBqsiDSBR9lgF-G1lwFdZszLtNf9ztTCgbaZOg/viewform) if you can help out with the event, the link has several areas where we need help.

**Transportation** – We encourage neighbors to walk or bike to the party. Limited street parking is available.
