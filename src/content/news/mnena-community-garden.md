Our Community Garden is being reborn! After 2 years sitting vacant the Marshall-New Era Neighborhood Association was able to secure a 5 year lease on the property at 26th and B Stret to re-start the local garden. The 44 plots were all snapped up quickly, but we keep a waiting list for those who are interested. If you’d like more information about the garden contact info@marshallnewera.org
