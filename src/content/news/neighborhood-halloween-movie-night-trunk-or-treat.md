The Boulevard Park Neighborhood Association (BPNA) and Marshall-New Era Neighborhood Association (MNENA) are joining to bring you a special Halloween event on Saturday, October 26th at the Marshall School!

Join us for a spooky trunk-or-treat replete with games, activities, and snacks, followed by a haunted outdoor (family-friendly!) movie night. Volunteers are needed! Please contact Noah Mebane at mebanen@gmail.com or 302-943-7445 if interested.

Feel free to invite your friends and neighbors.
