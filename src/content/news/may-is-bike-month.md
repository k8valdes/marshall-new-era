Marshall-New Era Neighborhood Association is taking part in **May is Bike Month!** By riding bikes and encouraging others to ride, we can make biking better in the Sacramento region AND have fun doing it.

We’re going to gather at Chris and Julia Smith’s home (615 27th St) at 10:00 am, then have a casual ride over to West Sacramento to join the Bike Festival: A free, family friendly event highlighting the health and safety benefits of cycling. Community partners, music, giveaways, a pump track, local bike tours, snacks and food truck will keep visitors engaged in West Sacramento’s Bridge District.

_Download the Love to Ride app and get involved!_
