You’re invited to CityConnect, our monthly mixer series! Join us every 3rd Thursday of the month to network with City staff, business owners, and fellow Sac residents. We’ll also be on-site to talk about our newest City programs! Admission & food will always be free to all attendees.

> **Where: The Lock & Key – Tapas + Bar (_2718 J St, Sacramento, CA 95816_) | [Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/D5dcTmcSDamDwvjk8)**
> 
> **When: Thursday, July 17th, 2025 from 5:30 – 8:30 PM**

**RSVP HERE: [https://forms.office.com/g/ptgDw33Fuu](https://forms.office.com/g/ptgDw33Fuu)**

This July, we’re celebrating and looking back on the latest season of **SacVibes**, our local government podcast that aims to educate, engage, and empower Sacramento residents. Haven’t listened to SacVibes? [Check out @ceteamsac on Youtube and Spotify!](https://www.youtube.com/@CETeamSac)

Please RSVP so we can get an accurate headcount of how many people to expect. Attendees are encouraged to bring informational flyers to share at our resource table! Food and admission are always free.

The Community Engagement Team is committed to meeting folks where they are in order to share important City resources. Your attendance and support would be greatly appreciated! We also encourage you to share this invitation with your networks. If you have any questions or if you have a recommendation for where we should host our next mixer, please email [ce@cityofsacramento.org](mailto:ce@cityofsacramento.org). 

To stay up-to-date on everything related to Community Engagement, subscribe to our [SacVibes vlog series](https://www.youtube.com/@CETeamSac) and follow us on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/ceteamsac/)!
