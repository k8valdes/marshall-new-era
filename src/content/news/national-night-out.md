**Tuesday, August 6th** – **6 pm to 9 pm**

Celebrate National Night Out with a progressive potluck dinner hosted by the Marshall-New Era and Boulevard Park Neighborhood Associations! Come out to chat with your neighbors, sample homemade goodies, and meet police officers, city officials and elected representatives.  
  
**Marshall-New Era neighbors will provide the desserts. Please bring a dish to share to  Marshall Park, at the corner of 28th and I, at 8 p.m. or sooner.** 

Celebrated on the first Tuesday in August, [National Night Out](https://fb.us17.list-manage.com/track/click?u=f15fc7d66ef8c326221bf4228&id=dbfc7f245b&e=25911ba1b8) began in 1984 with the goal of bringing communities together and strengthening relationships with local police. In 2017, more than 38 million neighbors gathered for block parties, parades and barbecues throughout the United States.

**Boulevard Park & Marshall-New Era Neighborhood Associations**  
**National Night Out Progressive Potluck Dinner**

**6-7 pm – Appetizers**  
Hors d’oeuvres course on the 22nd St. median, between Fat Alley and G Street   
   
**7-8 pm – Entrée**  
Entrée course in the courtyard outside the Hull and Allair home, 2431 H Street   
   
**8-9 pm – Desserts**  
Dessert course in Marshall Park, near the tot-lot (28th and I Streets)
