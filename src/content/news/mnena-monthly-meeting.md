MNENA Monthly Board Meeting We hope to see you at our next MNENA Board Meeting**,** scheduled for **Monday, July 21st**  at 6:00 PM. We’ll meet at the Society Church, 2711 B St, Sacramento (formerly the location of the B Street Theater). We’re grateful to Society Church for kindly offering their space for our meetings.  
  
Masks are optional, so feel free to wear one if it makes you more comfortable. There’s no need to RSVP—just come as you are!  
  
Our meetings are informal and typically begin with a small potluck. If you’d like, bring a finger food to share. The meeting will last 1 to 1.5 hours. You can review the Minutes from our [June Meeting](https://marshallnewera.us17.list-manage.com/track/click?u=f15fc7d66ef8c326221bf4228&id=da686c3916&e=51e76e1e0e) and the [July Agenda](https://marshallnewera.us17.list-manage.com/track/click?u=f15fc7d66ef8c326221bf4228&id=150b231b60&e=51e76e1e0e) in advance.  
  
We look forward to seeing you there!  
  
Warm regards,  
The MNENA Board
