Join us for a Meet & Greet with newly-elected District 4 Councilmember Phil Pluckebaum!

The Meet & Greet will be hosted on Wednesday, February 19 from 5:30 PM – 6:30 PM at the Washington Elementary School Cafeteria (520 18th Street). Light refreshments will be provided.

RSVP for this event at [https://www.boulevardpark.org/rsvp](https://www.boulevardpark.org/rsvp). RSVPs are not required to attend but will assist us in organizing this and future events.

Please feel free to distribute this event’ info to any neighbors who would be interested!

This event is brought to you by the Boulevard Park Neighborhood Association and Marshall-New Era Neighborhood Association.

We hope to see lots of our neighbors turn out for this one!
