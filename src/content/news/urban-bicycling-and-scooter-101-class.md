Join us for the City of Sacramento’s free Urban Bicycling and Scooter 101 Class!

The Urban Bicycling and Scooting 101 Class is a great learning opportunity for those interested in biking and scooting. This class discusses the laws around bicycling and scooting, traveling on street and through intersections, avoiding crashes, and most importantly how to enjoy the ride! 

Our next class will be held on **April 10th 6-7 pm,** in person, **City Hall First Floor, Room 1119.**

Can’t make it this time? Check our [calendar](https://www.cityofsacramento.gov/public-works/transportation/active_transportation/urban_biking_and_scooting_101_class) for future classes.

For questions, please send us an email at [**WalkBike@cityofsacramento.org**](mailto:WalkBike@cityofsacramento.org)
