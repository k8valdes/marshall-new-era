The Marshall New Era Neighborhood Association plans to clean up some of the traffic circles in our neighborhood, and plant new drought tolerant and native species plants later this fall. The first cleanup day will be Saturday Sept. 13th from 9am to 10:30am. We’ll meet at Marshall Park, at the corner of 27th and I Sts.

We’ll be starting with two traffic circles for this work event: at 25th and G Sts and 27th and F Sts. If enough volunteers step up to help, we’ll do additional traffic circles in the future.

Bring work gloves and water; we’ll provide garbage bags. Please RSVP to Mollie Quasebarth at molliequas@gmail.com if you can participate, so we can better plan our efforts.
