### Hope you can join us…

Get some exercise and meet your neighbors while caring for our community!  **WHEN** – Saturday December 9th 9:00 am-11:00am; coffee & pastries from 8:30am-9:00am.  **WHERE** – Leland Stanford Park, 27th and B Street  **HOW** – We will walk our neighborhood picking up trash. We have trash pickers available for use or you can bring your own. Trash bags and gloves will be provided.  We would love to have you come out and improve our neighborhood!   
  
In addition park staff staff will be there to plant trees and we are welcome to participate.  
  
**Invite your friends and neighbors to join us!  Share this email please.**
