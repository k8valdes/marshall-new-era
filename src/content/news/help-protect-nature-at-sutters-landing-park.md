The Sacramento City Council will vote on a new plan for Sutter’s Landing Park on **September 2nd.** The plan provides a great opportunity to designate the Park’s “Mound Area” and West Sutter’s Landing Park properties as “Wildlife Areas” and to eventually increase trail access to these areas to better connect the public with nature.  [Click here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1hjATDgNP6vkrM2wXMIFvWexjkUurRER7/view?usp=sharing) for more details in PDF format or [click here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1xjBfEtzvpUMrbps9hKboSEHqTiluoHPn/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=101508003249338103520&rtpof=true&sd=true) for a Word document with key points about the proposal.

Please contact Mayor McCarty  and your Councilmembers
