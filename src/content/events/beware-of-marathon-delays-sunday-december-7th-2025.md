The 42nd California International Marathon will close J Street (Carlson to Alhambra), Alhambra (J Street to Capitol Ave), and L Street (Alhambra to 15th) from about 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM; the Capitol area is closed roughly 3 AM to 4 PM.
