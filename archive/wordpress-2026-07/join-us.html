Join Us

MNENA members work to make this a better neighborhood by 

  • Advocating on your behalf with the City on Homelessness and other issues
  • Keeping you updated by the Police Department on Crime and Prevention
  • Meeting regularly with your Councilmember and Assemblymember or their staff
  • Tracking building permits so we can inform you of major projects
  • Quarterly Cleanup Events 
  • Notifying you via email and blog posts of community activities
  • and more

We’d love to have you join us as a voting member of MNENA. Membership is annual (January -December). Those who join after September 1st will have their membership extended through the following year. Dues are $ 20 for an individual/household, $ 35 for a business. Click this link if you are a renewing member for the payment option. New members should click for a membership application and follow the instructions.

If you’re not ready to join us yet, click here to get 2 or 3 emails per month on what is happening in the neighborhood.
